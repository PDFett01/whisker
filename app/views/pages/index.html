Welcome to the Derby City Whisker Club, a club for bearded men and those who love them.

Our Mission

To promote beardliness and brotherhood amongst men and the whiskerfication of the masculine image in the popular conscious.

A Little About Us

The Derby City Whisker Club (formerly Greater Louisville Society of Bearded Gentlemen) is the area's first "whisker club," a social group devoted to the appreciation of man's ability to grow hair on his face. To join this group, one need only be interested. Facial hair itself is not a prerequisite, and no person of any age, gender, race, nationality, or variance of hairiness shall be discriminated against by group leaders or administrators.
